Our Mission

Every person deserves respect, resources, and community. The R3SC honors the dignity of our neighbors experiencing homelessness.

What We Do

The R3SC actively serves unhoused community members by providing essential hygiene items, household goods, house-warming baskets, and meaningful human connection. We believe in meeting people where they are, with kindness and respect.
